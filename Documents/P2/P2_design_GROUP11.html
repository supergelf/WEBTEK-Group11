Administrative Details

Our imaginary client is a bowling alley/ recreation center called Bowlorama.

Purpose, goals and audience

Our purpose for the website is to make everyones bowling experience as simple as possible.

We want to establish an easy platform for those who want to enjoy a good round of bowling with friends and family.

The website will make it possible for visitors to book bowling alleys and pool tables, therefore making it easier for people to plan their actiieties beforehand.

The audience of the website are people who want to book a bowling alley, either for a small group of people, or a larger one like a birthday party. They will have the option to choose how many alleys, and if they plan on eating there as well.
The bowling alley also includes a section for playing pool, so people that are interested in that will be able to book pool tables.

The alley is marketed towards all ages with a particular emphasis on ages 13-20
