Our client

Our imaginary client is a bowling alley called BowlBros.

Purpose and goals

Our purpose and goal for the website is to make everyones bowling experience as simple as possible.

want to establish an easy platform for those who want to enjoy a good round of bowling with friends and family.

Audience

The audience of the website are people who want to book a bowling alley, either for a small group of people, or a larger one like a birthday party. They will have the option to choose how many alleys, and if they plan on eating there as well.
The bowling alley also includes a section for playing pool, so people that are interested in that will be able to book pool tables.

The content of the site and how it is organized

Front page

The front page will contain a header with links to all the other pages. It will include a main section with information about what the bowling alley can offer whether you are interested in coming as a large/ small group, throwing a birthday party, or just as an individual. The footer will include information about opening hours and ways to contact the bowling alley.

Events

The event page will contain special events coming up, like bowling and pool tournaments.

Reservations

This reservation page will contain ways to book bowling alleys or pool tables.

Prices

This page will include prices for bowling alleys and pool tables. It will contain information about prices for booking several bowling alleys and the discounts that apply.
It will also include information about a membership option.

Menu

The bowling alley site will include a restaurant section that will offer a selection of meals for individuals as well as for larger groups. This part of the site will only include information about what they offer, not the possibility for online food ordering.

Merch

The merch page contains information about all the merchandise the bowling alley offers. The page is only informational, so the customer has to buy these items physically in the bowling alley.

Contact Us

The contact us page will include the company email, phone number, and It willl also contain a form

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

Functional

  • The customer must be able to make reservations for both bowling and pool tables.
  • The customer must also have a user friendly way to contact the establishment.
  • The user must be able to buy merchandise from the website.

Non-functional

  • Easily get to the front page from every other page
  • The user should easily be able to access all relevant information about opening hours, prices and events
  • The website must have an appeling front page.
